Friction Formulas

1. Frictional Force (Ffriction)

Ffriction = μN

Where:

  • μ = Coefficient of friction (dimensionless)
  • N = Normal force (in Newtons, N)

2. Maximum Static Friction (Fmax)

Fmax = μsN

Where:

  • μs = Coefficient of static friction
  • N = Normal force (in Newtons, N)

3. Kinetic Friction (Fk)

Fk = μkN

Where:

  • μk = Coefficient of kinetic friction
  • N = Normal force (in Newtons, N)

4. Angle of Friction (θ)

tan(θ) = μ

Where:

  • μ = Coefficient of friction
  • θ = Angle of friction (in degrees or radians)

5. Work Done by Friction (W)

W = Ffriction × d

Where:

  • Ffriction = Frictional force (in Newtons, N)
  • d = Distance moved by the object (in meters, m)
