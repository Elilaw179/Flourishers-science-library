Molarity and Solutions Formulas

1. Molarity (M)

Formula: \( M = \frac{n}{V} \)

Where:

  • M: Molarity (moles of solute per liter of solution)
  • n: Number of moles of solute
  • V: Volume of the solution (in liters)

2. Number of Moles (n)

Formula: \( n = M \times V \)

Where:

  • n: Number of moles of solute
  • M: Molarity of the solution
  • V: Volume of the solution (in liters)

3. Dilution Formula

Formula: \( M_1V_1 = M_2V_2 \)

Where:

  • M1: Initial molarity of the solution
  • V1: Initial volume of the solution
  • M2: Final molarity of the solution after dilution
  • V2: Final volume of the solution after dilution

This formula is used to calculate the concentration of a solution after it has been diluted.

4. Mass of Solute (m)

Formula: \( m = n \times M_w \)

Where:

  • m: Mass of the solute (in grams)
  • n: Number of moles of the solute
  • Mw: Molar mass of the solute (in g/mol)

5. Preparing a Solution from a Solid

Formula: \( M = \frac{m}{M_w \times V} \)

Where:

  • M: Molarity of the solution
  • m: Mass of the solute (in grams)
  • Mw: Molar mass of the solute (in g/mol)
  • V: Volume of the solution (in liters)

6. Percent by Mass

Formula: \( \text{Percent by Mass} = \frac{\text{Mass of solute}}{\text{Mass of solution}} \times 100\% \)

Where:

  • Mass of solute: Mass of the solute in the solution (in grams)
  • Mass of solution: Total mass of the solution (solute + solvent) (in grams)

7. Percent by Volume

Formula: \( \text{Percent by Volume} = \frac{\text{Volume of solute}}{\text{Volume of solution}} \times 100\% \)

Where:

  • Volume of solute: Volume of the solute in the solution (in mL or L)
  • Volume of solution: Total volume of the solution (solute + solvent) (in mL or L)

8. Mole Fraction (χ)

Formula: \( \chi_A = \frac{n_A}{n_A + n_B} \)

Where:

  • χA: Mole fraction of component A
  • nA: Number of moles of component A
  • nB: Number of moles of component B (other components in the solution)

9. Molality (m)

Formula: \( m = \frac{n_{\text{solute}}}{\text{mass of solvent (in kg)}} \)

Where:

  • m: Molality of the solution (moles of solute per kg of solvent)
  • nsolute: Number of moles of solute
  • Mass of solvent: Mass of the solvent (in kilograms)
