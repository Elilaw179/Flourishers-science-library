Sound Waves Formulas in Physics

1. Speed of Sound

The speed of sound in a medium is given by:

Formula:

v = fλ

Where:

  • v = Speed of sound
  • f = Frequency of the sound wave
  • λ = Wavelength of the sound wave

2. Frequency and Period Relationship

The frequency of a sound wave is the reciprocal of its period.

Formula:

f = 1/T

Where:

  • f = Frequency of the sound wave
  • T = Period of the sound wave

3. Intensity of Sound

The intensity of sound is the power per unit area carried by the wave.

Formula:

I = P/A

Where:

  • I = Intensity of sound (W/m²)
  • P = Power of the sound wave (Watts)
  • A = Area through which the sound wave passes (m²)

4. Decibel Level

The decibel level of a sound measures its intensity relative to a reference intensity (I0).

Formula:

β = 10 log10(I/I0)

Where:

  • β = Sound level in decibels (dB)
  • I = Intensity of the sound wave (W/m²)
  • I0 = Reference intensity (usually 1 × 10-12 W/m²)

5. Doppler Effect

The Doppler effect describes the change in frequency of a wave in relation to an observer who is moving relative to the wave source.

Formula:

f' = f (v + vo) / (v - vs)

Where:

  • f' = Observed frequency
  • f = Actual frequency of the source
  • v = Speed of sound in the medium
  • vo = Speed of the observer relative to the medium (positive if moving towards the source)
  • vs = Speed of the source relative to the medium (positive if moving away from the observer)

6. Resonance in Pipes (Open and Closed)

Open Pipe (Both Ends Open)

Formula:

L = nλ/2

Where:

  • L = Length of the pipe
  • n = Harmonic number (1, 2, 3,...)
  • λ = Wavelength of the sound wave

Closed Pipe (One End Closed)

Formula:

L = (2n - 1)λ/4

Where:

  • L = Length of the pipe
  • n = Harmonic number (1, 2, 3,...)
  • λ = Wavelength of the sound wave

7. Beat Frequency

When two sound waves of slightly different frequencies interfere, they produce a beat frequency.

Formula:

fbeat = |f1 - f2|

Where:

  • fbeat = Beat frequency
  • f1 = Frequency of the first sound wave
  • f2 = Frequency of the second sound wave
